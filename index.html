Our story

Currently living in the Sierra Nevada Mountains of Northern California, Matt grew up outside of Boston and was taken on many trips to old quarries in New England by his mom and dad as a kid. Years later the interest was rekindled upon visiting the Tucson gem show in 2001. He has been collecting ever since, and made many friends in the business.

If you collect enough rocks, eventually you will run out of space and need to pass a few of them back into the universe for others to enjoy. So after years of collecting these beautiful gems and minerals, he decided to make them, as well as many newer items, available to others through eBay and Amazon stores.

Ethically Sourced

From the beginning of collecting it has been very important to us who the stones come from and whose hands they have passed through. This industry provides a way for locals in many remote parts of the word to make a sustainable living. Some of the mines are very small and worked by hand. Others are much larger and are professional, life long ventures for many families. We focus on fostering long-term relationships that often yield interesting material from the southern part of the African continent, Peru, Brazil and many others.
